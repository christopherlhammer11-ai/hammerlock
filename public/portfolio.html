REAL WORKFLOW

How a task flows through the system.

Someone asks HammerLock: "Research competitors and draft a pitch deck."

hammerlock > "research competitors in the local AI space and draft a pitch deck"
# Strategist agent activated. Here's what happens under the hood:

HammerLock receives, routes to Strategist agent

The right agent is selected based on the request. Strategist handles competitive analysis and pitch decks.

Web-Scraper pulls competitor data

Hits competitor websites, pricing pages, press releases. Extracts structured text, not raw HTML.

Real-Time-Verifier checks sources

Validates that URLs are live, data isn't hallucinated, sources are real. Assigns trust scores.

Prompt-Condenser compresses context

All that scraped content gets compressed 40% before hitting the LLM. Same quality, fewer tokens, lower cost.

RecallMax pulls your persona

Your company name, market position, previous research — compressed long-term memory from your encrypted vault.

Tool-Use-Guardian wraps every API call

If OpenAI times out, it retries with Groq. If JSON comes back malformed, it auto-repairs. Nothing fails silently.

Pitch deck drafted, encrypted, stored locally

The result lands in your vault. AES-256 encrypted. Never touched the cloud. Your strategy stays yours.
